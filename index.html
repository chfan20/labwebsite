FAN Lab: inFrastructure, AI, and urban Nexus

The Mission of the FAN Lab, directed by Dr. Chao Fan in the School of Civil and Environmental Engineering and Earth Sciences at Clemson University, is to create new knowledge and methods in integrated intelligence to derive transformative solutions for global challenges at the nexus of human, infrastructure and natural environment under ever-changing climate conditions, towards sustainability, resiliency, equity and livability.
