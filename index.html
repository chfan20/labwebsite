Principal Investigator: Chao Fan

Dr. Chao Fan is an Assistant Professor at Clemson University in the United States. His research focuses on creating science-based solutions to address global challenges at the intersection of human, infrastructure, and the natural environment in the context of a changing climate. His work aims to help cities achieve sustainability, resilience, equity, and livability. Dr. Fan and his team have had dozens of their works featured in over 20 international news outlets. He serves as a reviewer for more than 50 peer-reviewed journals and is a member of program committees for various academic conferences. He has been invited to deliver over 50 academic talks at prestigious institutions worldwide, including Princeton University, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Johns Hopkins University, National University of Singapore, University of Hong Kong, and the University of California.
